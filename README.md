🌍 European Innovation & Sustainability Dashboard

> **An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing innovation performance, sustainability metrics, research ecosystem, and economic indicators across European countries.**

---

# 📖 Project Overview

Innovation and sustainability are becoming two of the strongest indicators of a country's long-term economic growth.

This project combines multiple European datasets into a single interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze relationships between:

- Innovation Performance
- Research & Development Investment
- Economic Growth
- Green Energy Adoption
- Carbon Intensity
- High-Tech Employment
- Research Ecosystem

The dashboard is divided into three analytical pages, each focusing on a different aspect of Europe's innovation landscape.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Compare innovation performance across European countries.
- Analyze the relationship between R&D investment and innovation.
- Explore sustainability indicators including carbon intensity and low-carbon electricity.
- Study research and talent ecosystem indicators.
- Build an interactive dashboard that supports country-wise analysis.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Higher R&D investment is positively associated with innovation.
- Countries with lower carbon intensity often perform better in sustainability.
- High-tech employment supports innovation growth.
- Scientific publications and intellectual assets indicate strong research ecosystems.
- Knowledge-intensive exports reflect advanced economic development.
